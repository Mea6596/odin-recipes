Fesenjan

Fesenjan, also known as Persian pomegranate and walnut stew

Ingredients

  • 2 large yellow onion sliced thin
  • 5 oz oil
  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 8 oz walnuts
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 cup pomegranate concentrate
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • May serve with rice

Directions

  1. Add walnuts to food processor and process until it urns a tan-colored paste.
  2. Add 1/4 cup cold water to running food processor. Continue until uniform.
  3. Fry the onions with the oil in a 6-qt pot until golden brown.
  4. Add chicken to the pot.
  5. Add walnut paste into the pot along with salt and pepper.
  6. Drizzle pomegranate concentrate into the pot.
  7. Bring to boil over medium heat.
  8. Reduce heat to medium low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  9. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered for an additional 1 hour 15 minutes or until sauce is thickened. Stir every 15 minutes.
  10. Optional--Serve over steamed rice
