Mashed Potatoes

Flavorful and buttery bowl of mashed potatoes

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs potatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves, minced
  • salt for taste
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • optional toppings:chives, green onions, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes in to cubes.
  2. Add enough water to a pot and add potatoes, garlic and 1 tbsp salt to water.
    Bring water to boil and cook for 10-12 minutes. Drain water.
  3. While potatoes or boiling, heat butter, milk, and 2 tsp salt until
    mixture is melted and well combined.
  4. After potatoes have been drained, return to pot and mash.
  5. Add butter mixture. Taste and season as needed.
  6. Optional--Add garnishments.
